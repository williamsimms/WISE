Welcome To A Real Smart Home

Here To Help

Protection
Your home is always looking out for you. Whilst you're asleep, it keeps watch, alerts you to any intruders and sounds an alarm in an emergency.
Personalised
Your smart home knows you. Our Smart Home greets the first person who returns home with a welcoming warmth It makes sure your always comfortable in every room, and saves energy in the ones that are not being used.
Helpful
Waiting for an important parcel to arrive? Your home will let you know when it's there. Keep forgetting to close the garage door? Your home will gently remind you and even close it for you eventually.
Lighting
Lighting with a wow factor. This house has the power to transform spaces and atmosphere; it offers an excellent way to create drama and contrast within your home.
Fire Protection
If smoke or a fire is detected by the house, your house will immeadiately alert you with flashing lights and a phone call. Emergency lights can guide residents to safety and blinds are open to passersby.
Flood Protection
Should a water leak be detected, your home will bring this to your attention so that you can act quickly to minimise any damage.
Energy Saving
Our Smart Home can intelligently monitor your home's electricity consumption as well as your electricity productuion. You can view these statistics directly from your smartphone at any time.
Control
We designed a beautiful app for the Smart Home which allows you to have control from any device. Use it for setting the desired temperature in the bathroom, selecting your favourite playlist in the kitchen or simply to see your home's energy consumption.
